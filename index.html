Privacy Policy for Taktus

Last updated: May 15, 2025

Taktus ("the App") is a professional metronome, tuner and audio recorder developed by Marc Beltran. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Information We Do Not Collect

Taktus does not collect, store, transmit or share any personal data. The App works entirely offline and does not connect to any servers.

Microphone Access

The App requests access to your device's microphone solely to enable the audio recording and tuner features. Audio recordings are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted elsewhere.

Data Storage

All data (settings, recordings) is stored locally on your device. We have no access to this data.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
